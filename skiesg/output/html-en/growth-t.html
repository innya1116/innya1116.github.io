11. Monitoring Supplier ESG Assessment

100% key supplier ESG due diligence rate, High score rate in ESG risk assessment of key suppliers : ▲25%

SK innovation sets the main goal of 'supplier ESG risk management' as developing 'sustainable supply chain and stable procurement'. To achieve this goal, we will identify and support high-risk suppliers and continuously work with them to foster a sustainable supply chain.

Key supplier ESG due diligence rate / High score rate

* (Reference) Criteria on high-scoring partners: Leader group on ESG regular evaluation criteria (Grades 1 and 2 in ESG evaluation)

Strategy

Advancing a sustainable supply chain by implementing a customized ESG consulting program based on the regular evaluation of suppliers
  • Providing in-person/online training customized for the respective ESG evaluation scores
  • Endorsing mid-and long-term ESG performance improvements through consulting services

12. Expanding Social Contributions at Global Sites

CSR Programs by global site
: 2 Corporate-wide + 1 Region-specific

SK innovation is committed to establishing a win-win partnership and expanding our CSR programs to the global sites and local communities. We will customize our social contribution programs and embark on collaborative models with each local community.

CSR Program status by global site

Strategy

Expanding social contribution by carrying out our CSR programs to global business sites
  • Scaling up current corporate-wide programs into global initiatives (Care programs for children with developmental disability, plogging, etc.)
  • Developing and implementing region-specific programs for the respective global operation sites based on the identification and analysis of social issues, such as recruiting and empowering the marginalized etc.

13. Strengthening Green Start-up Partnerships

Cumulative number of green start-up partnerships
: 100 by 2025

Companies play a pivotal role in alleviating pressing issues such as climate change and plastic waste. Transition to a low carbon society can be accelerated when start-ups with innovative solutions expand their businesses. SK innovation has set the goal of fostering 100 cumulative green start-up partnerships by 2025. From 2021 onwards, SK innovation aims to foster 15 to 20 start-ups every year.

Cumulative number of green start-up partnerships

(Unit: Cumulative Number of Start-ups)

Strategy

Developing collaborative business models between SK innovation subsidiaries and green start-ups and constantly contributing to the green startup ecosystem
  • Establishment of at least 15 to 20 collaborative projects annually to launch new business models
  • Regular monitoring of partnership performances and management of networks with start-ups
