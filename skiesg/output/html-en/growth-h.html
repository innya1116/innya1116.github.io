14. Enhancing Employee Happiness

SK innovation happiness index target: 75 by 2025

16. Reinforcing Diversity & Inclusion

D&I policies at a global level by 2025
Inclusion into Global D&I indices by 2025

SK innovation is committed to developing a global management system that enhances diversity and inclusion. We regularly monitor factors related to wage fairness, including performance evaluation and promotion, to ensure a discrimination-free workplace.

Diversity & Inclusion training completion rate

* From 2022 onwards, D&I training performances will cover our employees in all sites, using the newly-devised education programs.

Strategy

Establishing and implementing Diversity & Inclusion (D&I) management system at a global level
  • Improvement of convenience facilities for the disabled and women in business sites
  • Development and implementation of training programs including obligatory training
  • Collection of feedback from employees at all levels from our global sites
