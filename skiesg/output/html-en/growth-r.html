3. Reducing Scope 1&2 Emissions

Hydrocarbon Biz: △25% Compared to 2019,
Green Biz: △42% to BAU

SK innovation has established carbon targets in both our existing business and rapidly growing new business. By 2025, the company plans to achieve a 25% reduction against the 2019 baseline for hydrocarbon business such as oil refinery & petrochemical and 42% reduction compared to BAU for green business such as electric vehicle battery & materials.

4. Reducing Scope 3 Emissions

Financial Intensity target : 75% Reduction until 2030

SK innovation will reduce the carbon intensity based on the fixed assets (financial intensity) by 75% by 2030 against the 2019 baseline. Through continued management, SK innovation aims to reduce financial intensity by more than 90% by 2050.

* Please click 'Learn more' to find the definition of financial intensity
