Compliance with more than 90% of global information security measurement standards, 100% completion rate of information security training

SK innovation is dedicated to drawing up measures against cyber-attacks and any security-related threats which may arise due to rapidly increasing automation and digitalization of operations and expanded remote working caused by COVID-19. To make preemptive responses against external and internal information security threats, the company sets "reinforcement of information security risk management" as a core task, and instilling the importance of executing an appropriate information security program for information protection within the company. In addition, it set a mid- and long-term goal related to information security and protection of personal information and industrial secrets and established a detailed execution plan, which is currently in force.

SK innovation sets "achieving more than 90% of the domestic and overseas standard information security management indices" and "achieving 100% of the completion rate of information security-related training" as the goals for 2025. We will establish a global-level information security management system to block any cases of information leakage, misuse, and abuse. First, for internalizing an information security system that meets the domestic and international information security certification (ISMS/ISO27001) level, internal regulations and risk management level will be improved corresponding to domestic and overseas legal requirements, and security responsibility management culture will be strengthened through training and campaign programs towards employees. The company will encourage to accomplish the goal by monitoring information security indices, reforming accident response system by each business site. Through this effort, it plans to reinforce local responsiveness and readiness against cyber-infringements and information leakage.

Through this task, SK innovation anticipates reinforced sustainability in all business activities by successfully executing information security as a social responsibility that is closely related to various stakeholders, such as clients, employees, and stockholders.
